B — Passive Voice: Complete the sentences
Rewrite each active sentence in the passive. Fill in the gap with the correct passive form.
Passive voice reminder:  present: is / are + past participle  ·  past: was / were + past participle  ·  modal: can / must / may + be + past participle
In legal and tax English, use passive when the action matters more than the actor.
"Germany deducted tax" → Tax was deducted by Germany

was denied are exempted was incorporated can be revoked was deducted are not accepted

→ Withholding tax from the dividend before LuxHolding received the net amount.
💭 Past passive — what happened to the dividend payment?
Question 2

→ The refund application by the BZSt under Section 50d paragraph 3.
💭 Past passive — an official decision was made.
Question 3

💭 Past passive — a completed historical act.
Question 4

→ Dividends between EU parent and subsidiary from withholding tax under the Directive.
💭 Present passive — an ongoing legal rule.
Question 5

→ A refund if the authority later discovers abuse.
💭 Modal passive — can + be + past participle.
Question 6

→ Post-hoc substance improvements generally as evidence of the original facts.
💭 Present passive negative — a general legal principle.
