2. Reading: Dr. Hammond's Story

**'English for You'** was a popular TV show many years ago. It started in **1959**. The main teacher was **Dr. Colin Hammond**, a British man who lived in a small, simple house. The show helped people in the German Democratic Republic (DDR) learn basic English. Dr. Hammond showed his **daily routine**. In one episode, he **made breakfast** and **went to the market** to buy food. The lessons were short, about **15 minutes**, and only used simple words. People loved Dr. Hammond's polite way of speaking, and they learned fast because the lessons were very focused on **practical English**.
