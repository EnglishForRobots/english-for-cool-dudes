Activity 2: Read and Understand

The Story of My Last Shopping Trip

Last Saturday, I decided to do all my weekly shopping. First, I went to the supermarket. I grabbed a **shopping trolley** and went up and down every **aisle** to find everything on my list. I saw that my favorite cereal was **on sale**, so I grabbed three boxes! When I had everything, I waited in a long queue at the **checkout**. I paid with my credit card and received a long **receipt** with all my purchases.

When I got home, I decided to buy a new book online. I found the book on a popular website called Amazon and clicked the **"add to cart"** button. The website showed me the **shipping** cost and estimated delivery date. I also read a few **reviews** from other customers to make sure the book was good. After I paid, I got an email with a **tracking number** so I could follow the package. Online shopping is so convenient!

True or False?

Read the sentences below and decide if they are true or false based on the story.

1. The person went shopping on Sunday.
2. The cereal was not on sale.
3. The person paid for groceries with a credit card.
