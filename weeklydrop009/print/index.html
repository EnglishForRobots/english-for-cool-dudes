B — Fix the Mistake
Each sentence has ONE word that is wrong. Cross it out and write the correct word.
Tip: Think about the MEANING of the sentence. If a word doesn't make financial sense, it's probably the mistake. Use the word bank below to help.
Word bank — corrections
harder cash flow positive loss increase
Sentence 1
"SpaceX posted a profit of $4.9 billion last year, mainly due to the enormous costs of its AI expansion."
💡 If costs were enormous, did the company make money or lose it?
Sentence 2
"Tesla, on the other hand, has been cash flow negative for years and sits on a $45 billion cash reserve."
💡 Could a company build up $45bn in cash if it spent more than it earned?
Sentence 3
"A merger would allow Musk to reduce his control of more of the AI ecosystem, according to analyst Dan Ives."
💡 Why would a CEO want LESS control over a fast-growing area like AI?
Sentence 4
"Tesla's move from Delaware to Texas has made it significantly easier for shareholders to pursue lawsuits against the company."
💡 New laws require shareholders to own at least 3% of shares to sue — easier, or ...?
