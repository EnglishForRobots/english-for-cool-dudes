üó£Ô∏è 2. Key Phrases

These are the most important phrases to remember for ordering:

  • **To Order:** "**Can I have** a water, please?" (Most common, polite, and safe for beginners.)
  • **To Order (Alternative):** "**I'd like** a coffee." (Polite, but slightly more formal than 'Can I have'.)
  • **To Ask for the Bill:** "**Can I have the bill, please?**" or "**The bill, please.**"
  • **To Respond to the Waiter:** "Yes, please," or "**No, thank you.**"
