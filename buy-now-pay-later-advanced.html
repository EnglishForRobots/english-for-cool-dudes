💡 Did you know?

  • **BNPL** stands for "Buy Now, Pay Later" - a financial service that allows consumers to make purchases immediately and pay in installments over time, typically without interest.
  • The global BNPL market was valued at approximately **$120 billion** in 2021 and is expected to reach over **$3.98 trillion** by 2030, showing explosive growth in digital finance.
  • Klarna, one of the major BNPL providers, was founded in **Stockholm, Sweden** in 2005 and is now one of Europe's most valuable fintech companies, serving over **150 million customers** globally.

📊Reading Comprehension: BNPL Statistics LEVEL 2

📈 The Rise of Buy Now, Pay Later

The BNPL industry has experienced explosive growth in recent years. Transaction volume has multiplied 20 times since 2019, representing a dramatic shift in consumer payment preferences.

Major providers typically charge merchants as much as 5% of the transaction value, while 90-plus percent of their income comes from merchants rather than consumers. Affirm reports that about 20% of their business today is 0% interest, while 80% carries interest charges.

However, there are growing concerns about consumer debt. A LendingTree survey found that 41% of BNPL users paid late in the past year, up from 34% the year before. Additionally, 25% of customers have used BNPL for groceries, up from 14% in 2024.
