Mr. Jones was annoyed because he had a very tight **connecting flight** in Rome. The agent suggested he speak to the customer service desk to see if they could re-book him on an earlier flight, but Mr. Jones decided to simply wait at the **departure gate**, hoping the fog would clear quickly.
