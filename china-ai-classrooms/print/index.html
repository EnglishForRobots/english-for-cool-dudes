🧠 AI in the Classroom — Practice Sheet

A fun 5-minute warm-up or wind-down for "Smart Schools or Surveillance?" · Upper-Intermediate

1️⃣ Spy Word Match

Draw a line (or write the number) connecting each word to its sneaky meaning.

  • 1. surveillance
  • 2. consent
  • 3. alarmed
  • 4. glimpse
  • a) a quick, partial look
  • b) freely given permission
  • c) worried something is wrong
  • d) close watching/monitoring

2️⃣ Finish the Thought

Use your imagination — there's no wrong answer here!

  1. If my school gave me a brainwave headband, I would...
  2. The strangest thing about AI in classrooms is...
  3. In ten years, schools will have...

3️⃣ Quick Grammar Snack

Circle the correct word.

  1. If schools (explain / explained) the tech better, parents would understand more.
  2. The data (is sent / sends) directly to a chat group for parents.
  3. By 2030, AI tools (will have become / become) completely normal in classrooms.

4️⃣ 60-Second Debate Starter

Grab a partner (or talk to your coffee cup ☕). Pick one side and argue it for 60 seconds, no stopping!

"Schools should use AI to monitor concentration." → AGREE or DISAGREE?

🗣️ Bonus points for using "surveillance," "consent," or "alarmed" while you talk!

5️⃣ Doodle Break 🎨

Design your own "future classroom gadget." Give it a silly name and one sentence describing what it does.
