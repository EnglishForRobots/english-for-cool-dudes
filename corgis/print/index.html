Past simple reminder:  Regular verbs: add -ed → walk → walked · cook → cooked
Irregular verbs: learn them! → go → went · take → took · sleep → slept
We use past simple for actions that are finished in the past.

C — Present → Past: Rewrite the sentences
Each sentence is in the present tense. Rewrite it in the past simple. Fill in the gap.
How to rewrite in past simple:  "The Queen loves her corgis." → "The Queen loved her corgis."
"They live in a palace." → "They lived in a palace."
Remember: irregular verbs change completely — have → had · eat → ate · are → were
