💬 Activity 2: Conversation Reading & Comprehension

Maria: Hello! I am Maria. I am from Spain, so I am Spanish. Where are you from?

Kenji: Hi Maria! My name is Kenji. I am from Japan, so I am Japanese.

Maria: That's cool. Do you like sushi?

Kenji: Yes, I love it! It's delicious. What about you? Are you here for a holiday?

Maria: Yes, I am. The people here are very kind. I am learning English here.

Kenji: Me too! Good luck with your studies!
