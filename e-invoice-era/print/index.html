B — Modal Verbs: Choose the correct form
Complete each sentence with the correct modal of obligation from the word bank.
Modal verb reminder:
must / have to → a real legal obligation. "All companies have to receive e-invoices."
don't have to → no obligation, but allowed. "Small companies don't have to send them yet."
can → permission or possibility. "A supplier can still send paper invoices if the customer agrees."
