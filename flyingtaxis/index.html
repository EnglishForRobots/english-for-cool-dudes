üõ∞Ô∏è May, Could & Will

When discussing future technology, we use different modals to show how certain we are about a prediction.

Will (Certainty): Flying taxis will become a daily mode of transport.
May / Might (Possibility): That reality may not be so far away.
Could (Theoretical Potential): This could reduce traffic in big cities.
