C — Word Match

1. source country
A tax-free amount you can earn from renting before paying income tax
2. DAC7
Platforms automatically sending host earnings to tax authorities
3. letting allowance
The country where the rented property is located
4. platform reporting
A bilateral agreement that decides which country taxes which income
5. tax treaty
EU directive (2023) requiring platforms to report host income to tax offices
6. double taxation
When the same income is taxed in full by two different countries
