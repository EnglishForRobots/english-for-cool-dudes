B — Grammar: Tenses & connectors
Write the correct word or phrase in each gap.
Tense review: Present simple — facts and how things work in general. Past simple — completed events in the past. Present perfect — past action with present relevance.
Connectors: despite + noun/gerund · even though + subject + verb · without + noun/gerund
