Pre-Watching Comprehension & Dictionary

Read the following summary based on the video's content before watching. Tap or click on the bolded words to see their definitions.

The rise of Artificial Intelligence is fueling a massive economic boom, necessitating a huge physical expansion of data centres in places like Lowden County, Virginia. These centers are the physical underpinnings of AI models, consuming such vast amounts of energy that capacity is measured in 'megawatts' and 'gigawatts'. The striking reality is that a single server rack can suck up more power than a small village, making the operation extremely energy-intensive.

This technological surge, marked by surging valuations, has led to warnings from institutions like the IMF. They observe echoes of the 'dot-com bubble', raising the question of what happens if the AI boom goes bust. The economic impact is clear: when investment in these centers is removed, US GDP growth comes close to recession territory. For now, massive investments, sometimes deemed gobsmacking, are being poured into this uncharted territory, but whether this capital expenditure will truly pay off is hard to predict.
