üì∞ Reading: Dancing Goats

Long ago in Ethiopia, a man named Kaldi watched his goats. One day, he saw that the goats were very active. They danced and did not want to sleep.

Kaldi discovered that the goats ate small red berries from a tree. He tried the berries too. Suddenly, he felt lots of energy!

He took the berries to a monk. At first, the monk threw them into a fire. But soon, the berries smelled wonderful. They mixed the roasted berries with water, and the first cup of coffee was born!
