B — Grammar: Present simple & past simple
Write the correct form of the verb in brackets.
Present simple: Use for facts that are always true. He/she/it → add -s. "The song means good enough."
Past simple: Use for finished actions. Regular → -ed. Irregular → must learn: make → made, write → wrote, become → became.
