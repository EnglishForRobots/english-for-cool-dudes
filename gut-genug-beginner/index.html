A German song — and the whole world loves it!
"Gut genug" is a German song. The name means "good enough" in English. Three artists made the song together: KitschKrieg, Blumengarten and Shirin David. The song is very popular on the internet. Millions of people share it on TikTok and Instagram every day — even people who don't speak German!
· · ·
Why do people love it?
The melody is simple and beautiful. It is easy to sing along to. The words say: "You are good enough." This is a happy message for everyone. Many English speakers mishear the words. They think it says "Doobie Scoot Canoe" — which is very funny! The song also gets help from algorithms. Apps like TikTok and Instagram show the song to more and more people when they like and share it.
· · ·
Four words. The whole world. You are good enough.
German TV star Heidi Klum wrote a comment about the song online. She said: "I love only his part." This was strange! Some people were not happy with her. Shirin David's part of the song is about feeling pressure — the feeling that you must be perfect. The chorus repeats: "Du bist gut genug." Four simple German words are now travelling around the world with a beautiful message: You are good enough.
