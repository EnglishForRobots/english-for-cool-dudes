1. Reading: Setting Career Goals üéØ

The annual Performance Review is the key meeting for discussing your career path. This is where you reflect on your past achievements and set future objectives.

To successfully negotiate a pay rise or promotion, you must clearly demonstrate how you have exceeded expectations in your current role. You should bring clear evidence, measured by specific metrics such as sales targets or efficiency gains, to your meeting.

Career progression often involves a Job Description change and a move to a higher Pay Grade. You may also be asked to mentor junior colleagues as part of your development plan.

A good review focuses on strengths and weaknesses, giving you constructive feedback for professional development.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) are the high-level metrics used to track progress toward your strategic objectives.
