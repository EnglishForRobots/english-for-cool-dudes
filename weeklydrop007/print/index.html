B — Collocations: Complete the table
Match the verb on the left with the correct noun phrase to make a natural English collocation from the story.
What is a collocation? Two or more words that naturally go together. We say make an impact, not do an impact. We say reach €1,500, not arrive €1,500. Learning collocations is one of the fastest ways to sound natural in English.
Noun phrases — use each once
an impact on out of the market as an example in on a basic human need €1,500 per month
Verb / phrase Complete the collocation Meaning
reach Prices or figures arrive at a level
priced Costs are too high to afford
cash Make profit from an opportunity
stand Serve as a symbol or model
make Have a real effect on something
serve as Function as something essential
